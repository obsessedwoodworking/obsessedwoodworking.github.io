Best Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors in 2023: Protecting and Beautifying Your Cedar Wood

June 29, 2023 · 4 min · 811 words · Barbara Birch

  • 2. Ready Seal 112 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors

  • 5. Seal One 7614 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors

Cedar wood is a popular choice for outdoor projects due to its natural beauty and its ability to resist bacteria and fungus. However, to ensure its longevity and protect it from insects, mold, and mildew, it is necessary to apply a clear cedar finish. In this article, we will explore the five best clear cedar finishes for outdoors in 2023 and discuss their features, pros, and cons.

1. Minwax 63210444 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors#

One of the top choices for clear cedar finishes is the Minwax 63210444. This exterior finish is specially formulated to protect cedar wood from moisture and UV rays. It contains unique oils that allow the finish to stretch and shrink with the wood, ensuring long-lasting durability. The stain is versatile and can be used on a wide range of surfaces, including furniture, doors, windows, countertops, and more. It provides a semi-gloss finish that enhances the natural beauty of the wood. To achieve optimal results, it is recommended to thoroughly mix the stain before use and continuously stir it during application.

Pros:

  • Easy to use on different surfaces
  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Provides UV protection
  • Preserves the natural beauty of the wood

Cons:

  • May yellow on certain projects

2. Ready Seal 112 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors#

Ready Seal 112 is a user-friendly option for exterior wood. With this product, there is no need for priming or wet lining application. It can be used directly from the can and does not require thinning. This ready-to-use formula makes it convenient and hassle-free to apply. The stain develops its true color after two weeks, allowing for a deeper and more authentic finish. It is ideal for outdoor furniture and cedar decks, providing protection against moisture and UV rays. Surface preparation is essential for professional results, and it is recommended to remove any previous coatings before application.

Pros:

  • Easy to use and does not require thinning
  • Self-priming and saves time
  • Mildew resistant

Cons:

  • Pricey, but worth the investment

3. Cabot 140.0003400.005 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors#

Cabot 140.0003400.005 is a top choice for those looking for a clear cedar finish with penetrating oils. This finish creates a protective barrier against the elements and is suitable for patio furniture and other outdoor cedar fixtures. It is highly recommended for cedar surfaces, as it brings out the natural beauty of the wood. The stain provides maximum UV protection and helps make exterior projects mildew resistant. Surface preparation is crucial for the best results, and it is important to ensure the wood surface is free of grime and dirt before applying the product.

Pros:

  • Works as advertised and provides durability
  • Suitable for different surfaces
  • Offers optimal UV protection

Cons:

  • Pricey, but the quality justifies the cost

4. Sabre Systems 300517 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors Deck Premium#

Sabre Systems 300517 is a two-in-one semi-transparent deck stain that is specifically designed for soft woods, including cedar. This stain not only protects the wood from fading and moisture but also amplifies its natural beauty. It is a low VOC stain, meaning it emits no strong odors and is safe for pets, humans, and kids. Cleanup is easy with just soap and water, and it does not require the use of mineral spirits. The stain can be applied to damp wood, saving time between cleaning and staining. It is available in five stunning colors, allowing for customization to match your project.

Pros:

  • Simple soap and water cleanup
  • Works well on damp wood
  • Provides an excellent uniform finish

Cons:

  • Specially formulated for soft woods

5. Seal One 7614 Clear Cedar Finish for Outdoors#

Seal Once Marine Penetrating with Sealer is a waterproof cedar preservative that offers both natural aesthetics and protection against severe outdoor conditions. It utilizes nanotechnology to deeply penetrate the wood and protect it from mildew, rot, water damage, and sun damage. This finish allows water vapor to escape, preventing trapping beneath the surface. It provides maximum UV protection and is safe for the environment. It is suitable for marine settings and can be used in and around fresh and saltwater. Additionally, it offers 11 color shades, serving both as a clear finish and a stain.

Pros:

  • Durable and protects against severe outdoor conditions
  • Applicable on various surfaces
  • Offers UV protection and prevents premature aging

Cons:

  • May not work well with wood glue

In conclusion, when it comes to protecting and enhancing the beauty of your outdoor cedar projects, choosing the right clear cedar finish is crucial. The five options discussed in this article, including Minwax 63210444, Ready Seal 112, Cabot 140.0003400.005, Sabre Systems 300517, and Seal One 7614, all provide unique features and benefits. Consider the specific requirements of your project, such as surface type and desired finish, to make an informed decision. By applying a high-quality clear cedar finish, you can ensure the longevity and beauty of your cedar wood for years to come.
